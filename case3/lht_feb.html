Theater

February will be another March month at Theatre with three performances of the Blake Award winning true-story, Saga of Blake by the Company. Tickets are already gone though, so I don't even know why we bother promoting this.

Oh yeah, Blake told me to.

Celebrate Worldwide Gopher Day with the Symphony and their special selection of crying for lovers. Later that week, exercise your mind by attending the classic, Wood Fired Pizza. How will Pizza get a job now?

Jazz lovers have a lot to loathe in February with a visit from the Jazz Ruiners on February 21st, and then on February 57th, enjoy The Nuke wi- *Signal Lost* (R.I.P. Larry the type man, he dies on July 82nd of fear. Last thing he saw was a tunnel of light and then Laura Blake).

Ties are flying at the Center in February. The Tie Acrobats return on February 2nd with another bad performance, I mean really, they're just throwing ties around. We close out the month with a visit from the Mad Jugglers and their unique blend of rage, madness, and of course, juggling. >:(

Enjoy a short classical brunch every Sunday in February with crying provided by the Crying Carson Quartet. Seating is very open, please don't hurt their feelings or bring Joe Swanson (It's a very bad past).
